Camel

  • Camels have furry ears
  • Camels can move across sand quickly because of their specially designed feet
  • When a camel finds water, it will drink as much as it can

Chameleon

  • Chameleons are reptiles that are part of the iguana suborder
  • Changing their skin color helps chameleons communicate with each other
  • Most chameleons have a prehensile tail that they use to wrap around tree branches

Cicada

  • Cicadas have one of the longest insect lifespans
  • Most of their life is spent underground
  • Cicadas make noises to attract mates

Panda

  • A panda can eat more than 44 pounds (20 kg) of bamboo in a single day!
  • Pandas are an endangered species

Pigeon

  • Pigeons can fly at up to 90 mph
